About Me

I’m Sana Shafique, a passionate Software Quality Assurance Engineer with hands-on experience in testing and automation across multiple domains, including ERP Systems, Healthcare Platforms, AI-based Applications, Learning Management Systems (LMS), Hotel & Booking Management, and Airline Management Systems.
